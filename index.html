MACHETES

2 feet Quesadillas! Big tortilla handmade from authentic, organic and ancestral Mexican corn stuffed with different stews of fresh and delicious ingredients. All dishes are accompanied by salsa (hot, medium or mild), Pico de Gallo and limes.

ITACATES

Itacate comes from the Nahuatl word "Itacatl". They are corn triangles stuffed with a vegetable stew (beans and avocado leaves), mixed with your choice of stew below. Accompanied with lettuce, pickled onions, cheese, salsa and sunflower seeds. All dishes are accompanied by salsa (hot, medium or mild), Pico de Gallo and limes.
